Fanart Frenzy

Create all kinds of art for your favorite fanfiction, encourage fellow artists, fight to eradicate the art commission scammers, and liven up our fandoms!

Fanart Frenzy is a month-long, multi-fandom bingo event designed to bring a wave of fanfiction-inspired fanart into the community. Free and unsolicited, as fandom is supposed to be, with real, human-made art only!

Check out our About and FAQ pages for more information, or take a quiz to see what kind of art you should make!
