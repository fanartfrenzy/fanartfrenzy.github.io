Posting Guidelines

Tumblr Reddit bluesky AO3

Here’s a quick guide on what to include in your posts! This will vary slightly by platform. Post anywhere you’d normally post - these are just guidelines for the socials we use and are aware of. If you’re only able to post on one platform (ex. if you’ve made a tumblr-style gifset), please feel free to link it on our other socials as well! Please include all elements in your posts, but beyond that, design and structure your posts however you like.

Tumblr:

Elements to include in your post:

1. The art!

  • - Instructions and tips for posting can be found here: images, videos, audio. If your art can’t be shared by any of these methods, send us a message and we’ll help you sort it out.
  • - For longer videos, we recommend posting on youtube, vimeo, or another video hosting site and posting as a link/embedding.
  • - For audio such as music or podfics, we recommend posting on an audio hosting platform and posting as a link/embedding.
  • - If your art can’t be shared by any of these methods, or if you’re having trouble posting, please send us a message and we’ll help you sort it out!

2. Image/Video description

  • - Alt-text for any images, video, or audio included in your post. This makes it easier for people using screen readers to enjoy your art.

3. Credit the Author

  • - A link to the fic that inspired your work as well as the fic’s title, author, tags, and summary.
  • - Optional: If you like, share a little about the fic, why you enjoyed it, what inspired you, etc. to recommend it to others.

4. Warnings

  • - If any archive warnings apply to the fic or your art, please mention that in your post in clear terms.
  • - Do not use euphemisms or alternative terms (ex. grape) or censor words (ex. r*pe). Using the exact terms helps people using tumblr’s content blocking mechanism avoid content they don’t wish to see.

5. Tagging

  • - If the author is on tumblr, tag them by adding @ username, or use their hashtag. If not, please share the post with them via link.
  • - Tag us! @FanartFrenzy and use the hashtags #fanart frenzy or #fanartfrenzy
  • - Note which bingo square you’re filling in. If you’d like to include your bingo card in the post, instructions for including additional images here, and for placing it behind a cut here.

Archive of Our Own:

Elements to include in your post:

1. The art!

  • - Ao3 does not natively host image, audio, or video content
  • - We recommend posting on an appropriate platform and embedding on Ao3. Instructions on how to do that can be found here: Images, Audio/Video

2. Image/Video description

  • - Please include a description of any image, video, or audio
  • - You can add alt text to an image embed by adding alt text to your image embed code like so:
  • -
  • - This is not an option for video embeds. Consider including a video description in the author’s note instead 

3. Give it a title

  • - Ao3 does require a work title, but it doesn’t have to be anything fancy! A common titling method is something like “[Art/Moodboard/Playlist/Etc] for [Inspiration Fic]”

4. Tags

  • - Ao3 requires that you select a Fandom, Language, Rating, and Archive Warning
  • - Consider tagging your art with its medium (fanart, playlist, moodboard, tradional/digital art, fanvid, etc) as well as any other relevant tags

5. Add your art to our Ao3 collection!

  • - Option 1: Go to the collection page and select “Post to Collection”. This will auto-fill the collection information in the appropriate box
  • - Option 2: Fill in the collection information yourself by typing Fanart Frenzy 2026 in the “Post to Collections/Challenges” box (at the top of the Associations section) and select our collection

6. Use the inspired by function

  • - In the Associations section, there is a checkbox saying “This work is a remix, a translation, a podfic, or was inspired by another work”. Check it and fill in the details for connecting your art to its inspiring fic
  • - For works hosted on Ao3, all you need it the work URL and Ao3 will auto-fill the details for you
  • - For works hosted elsewhere, you must fill out the URL, title, and author attribution yourself
  • - This function means your art will link back to the inspiration fic in the notes section of your art, and if the author approves the link on their end, their fic will link to your art!

7. Gift your art

  • - Please consider using the gift function if the author is on Ao3. Simply type their pseud into the “Gift this work to” box in the Associations section and select them.
  • - This results in the “[Work] by [You] for [Author]” style work title you may have seen in search results
  • - Some authors have set their preferences to not receive gifts, in which case Ao3 will give you an error message telling you this when you go to post. You’ll have to remove the gift attribution in this case

 

Note: While you are free to post on X/Twitter, please refrain from posting links to that platform on our subreddit or on our discord server. If you would like your art from Twitter on either of those platforms, please create a new post rather than linking from Twitter.
